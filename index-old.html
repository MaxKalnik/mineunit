Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mineunit?

The Mineunit platform is an infrastructure project for both consumers and businesses.

About the company

Mineunit is a group of companies established by professionals from different areas, including blockchain, cryptocurrencies, highload projects, marketing and investments, to bring a whole new experience for different market players. Firstly, we wish to simplify the mining process and give the ability to open the cryptocurrency world to those without prior knowledge. Secondly, we adopt a distributed computing business model as we believe this industry has a bright future ahead, and we plan to compete with industry leaders such as AWS, Google Cloud and Microsoft Azure in different areas, and we also aim to beat their prices and democratize computations with GPU acceleration. Our strategy idea is to popularize cryptocurrencies and help move the whole world forward to what we consider to be the Economics 2.0 concept.

About mineunit

The Mineunit company is the door into cryptocurrencies and the blockchain world, and mineunit is the key. It is the first device with a ‘plug and earn’ concept, giving our users the ability to earn money without much effort and without spending many hours learning the basics of mining while trying to avoid scam offers. In designing the device, we are working hard to make it be accessible to a typical household with children and pets. This is our main objective in engineering.

Have you finalized the mineunit designs?

No, we are still in the R&D stage and working on the final concept for the hardware. It is possible that new GPUs will soon be coming to the market and if this is the case, we wish to make use of the latest technology and the advantages they will bring.

Software platform

The Mineunit platform plans to provide an easy, yet powerful experience for our users: the ability to earn money on autopilot from cryptocurrency mining and distributed computing, to spend the money right from a mobile application on our partners’ goods and services with discounts for our users, and to cashout money with a debit card. Of course, you will be able to save money in targeted currencies and sell it on high positions to earn even more money. Our core system is designed for maximum profit, where AI choose what coin should be mined at a given time, and this process is changed automatically - no user interaction is needed at all. Afterwards, all altcoins are exchanged to target cryptocurrencies (for example Bitcoin or Ethereum) or fiat money.
