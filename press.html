Spacing and Usage

The Mineunit combination mark should always be surrounded by a minimum area of space. The length of the logomark serves as basis for setting the minimum area of space around the combination mark, as illustrated on the right.

Clear Space

Width of the logomark is X; approximate minimum area of space around the logo = X2 .

Please, don’t…

The five following figures illustrate the incorrect usage of the Mineunit logomark. The logomark should have the right dimensions, and it should adhere to the colors implemented by the brand.

  • Do not rotate, transform, distort, or alter in other ways
  • Do not add stroke, drop shadow, or any other effects
  • Do not use against blue background
  • Do not recolor
